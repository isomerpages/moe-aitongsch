Students are guided through different techniques of drawing, colouring and 3D sculpturing.

Some techniques of drawing include using lines, shapes, texture, colour and perspective. Basic techniques of colouring include colour schemes and blending of colours using different medium. Basic techniques of 3D sculpturing involve the use of various media such as clay and paper mache to create simple 3D objects. These techniques are taught in Art Club to build up studentsâ€™ foundation in Art and Crafts.
